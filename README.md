## Overview

Harmony is an intelligent workspace designed around the web.

Built around a modern browser foundation, Harmony combines thoughtful design, intelligent automation, and privacy-first principles to create a seamless environment for browsing, researching, writing, coding, and getting work done.

Rather than overwhelming users with countless features, Harmony focuses on reducing digital friction—allowing technology to quietly assist while people stay focused on what truly matters.

The browser is only the foundation.

Harmony's goal is to become a workspace that feels calm, capable, and genuinely helpful.

---

## Vision

Technology should simplify work, not complicate it.

Harmony is being built to feel less like software and more like an intelligent companion—one that understands context, automates repetitive tasks, respects user privacy, and stays out of the way until it's needed.

Our long-term vision is simple:

> **Create software that quietly makes every digital task feel easier.**

---

## Design Principles

Every decision made in Harmony follows four non-negotiable principles.

- **Never interrupt the user.**
- **Never slow down the computer.**
- **Never upload user data without explicit permission.**
- **Never make the user do something Harmony can do for them.**

These principles guide every product, design, and engineering decision.
